Over the past 15 years, I’ve walked a meaningful path in the music industry
as a singer, guitarist, stage performer, and behind-the-scenes contributor for numerous artists.
Each role has shaped my journey, leaving behind traces of tears,
creating smiles, and bringing joy to those who have shared in these moments, however briefly.

I stand proud and grounded in the musical path I’ve chosen and continue to cherish.

Thank you
for every word of encouragement, every act of support,
and every bit of strength that has carried me forward with pride and purpose.

FongJun Wanatchaporn Phaholyut

Singer & Guitar | Performer | Guitar Teacher


(Born February 11, 1996 – Bangkok, Thailand)

FongJun is a talented Thai artist and rising entrepreneur. She holds a Bachelor of Business Administration in Business Computer of Digital from Bangkok Thonburi University.


With a versatile background, she is not only known as a singer, actress, and songwriter, but also as a dedicated music teacher and behind the scenes creative in music production. Her signature vocal style powerful yet elegantly refined has earned her recognition as a role model for the new generation of aspiring artists pursuing a career in music.


With over 15 years of experience in the music industry and continuous live performances across the country, FongJun has established herself as a respected artist of substance, known for her heartfelt storytelling and authentic expression through every note she sings.
